About IIIT Surat

The Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Surat was established in 2017 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, under the Public Private Partnership (PPP) model.

Located at Kholvad Campus, Kamrej, Surat, Gujarat, IIIT Surat is built on a sprawling 28.71-acre campus that opened in September 2022. The institute is mentored by SVNIT Surat and has industry partners including GNFC, GGL, and GIL.

Our mission is to become a world-class institution in technical education, research, and innovation, contributing to India's technological advancement and economic growth.

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution for excellence in information technology education and research.

Mission

To nurture innovative minds and create leaders in technology through quality education and cutting-edge research.

Welcome to IIIT Surat, where innovation meets excellence in technical education.

As the Director of IIIT Surat, I am proud to lead an institution that stands at the forefront of technological advancement and educational excellence. Since taking charge in June 2024, I have witnessed firsthand the dedication of our faculty, the enthusiasm of our students, and the commitment of our industry partners.

Admissions

Admission Process

Admissions to IIIT Surat are based on JEE Main scores for B.Tech programs and GATE scores for M.Tech programs.

B.Tech Admission
  • JEE Main qualified candidates
  • Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling
  • Total seats: 180
M.Tech Admission
  • GATE qualified candidates
  • CCMT counseling process
  • Programs: AI & Data Science, VLSI Design
